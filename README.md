## 🧠 How I Think About CTI

Good intelligence isn't just about collecting IOCs. It's about asking the right questions - *who is this actor, why are they targeting this sector, what does their infrastructure tell us about their next move?* My background in recruitment taught me to read people, understand organisations, and communicate findings clearly to different audiences. I hope those skills transfer well.
